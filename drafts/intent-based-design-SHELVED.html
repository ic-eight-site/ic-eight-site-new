Intent-Based Design: The Principle Behind IC Eight's Localization Framework

The principle underneath AI-Native Localization Framework, Intent-Based Generation, and the Four-Layer Workflow. · Published 2026

What is Intent-Based Design?

Intent-Based Design is the design principle behind IC Eight's localization framework. It extracts intent from the source text, then designs the target language from that intent. The source is material, not the structure to preserve.

Design decisions — structure, order, tone, and level of explanation — follow from that intent, not from the shape of the source.

How IC Eight implements Intent-Based Design

AI-Native Localization Framework

AI-Native Localization Framework is IC Eight's implementation of Intent-Based Design for localization, with Japanese product UX as its primary focus. It is a system for generating localized UX and content from intent, product context, constraints, and validation criteria, rather than translating source text sentence by sentence.

Intent-Based Generation

Intent-Based Generation is the core method inside the Framework. It extracts the intent of the content — its purpose or goal — and generates language from that intent, instead of translating source text line by line.

Four-Layer Workflow

The Four-Layer Workflow is the operational model that makes Intent-Based Generation controllable: intent, generation, evaluation, and judgment, kept separate so AI-generated localization remains reviewable at every step.

Frequently asked questions

Is Intent-Based Design the same as AI translation?

No. AI translation begins with source text and asks how to express it in another language. Intent-Based Design begins by extracting intent from the source, then designs the target language from that intent.

How is Intent-Based Design different from the AI-Native Localization Framework?

Intent-Based Design is the principle behind it. The AI-Native Localization Framework is how IC Eight applies that principle, with Japanese product UX as its primary focus.

Where does Intent-Based Generation fit in?

Intent-Based Generation is the core method inside the Framework — the technique of extracting intent and generating language from it.
